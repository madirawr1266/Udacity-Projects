Healer's Log: April 15th, 2024

Today I started a new character. A warrior of the light, known as a Holy Paladin. I've donned my armor and joined my friends. We are going to try to clear a dungeon called The Nokhud Offensive.

So far the dungeon is going well. We were a little confused in the beginning. Who would have thought that this dungeon requires us to fly to the boss. I love to dragonride. So seeing this in the dungeon was a pleasant surprise.

There he was . . . Granyth, the fearsome dragon that has been terrorizing the natives.

The dragon was no easy foe. He gave us a quite a bit of trouble. The damage from this dragon was insanse! Whenever he casted Eruption we would struggle to survive. I wasn't able to heal through the damage the boss was putting out. Self doubt set in. I was really feeling like a failure. But then, one of my team members clicked on a nearby catapult and launched a lance in the boss's direction. STUNNING THE BOSS! Needless to say, Granyth won't be giving us any more trouble.

Overall, this encounter was a struggle. But I did learn something new. Sometimes it's not my fault when my teammates die. It may be just a click of a button that saves the day. Note to self: study the mechanics of each fight before engaging in battle.
